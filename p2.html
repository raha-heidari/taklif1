Each breed of Dog has its own characteristics, needs and behaviors, so choosing a breed depends on your lifestyle so we are here to help you.

Your guide in choosing a dog breed

Labrador retriever

A friendly and social breed known as guide and companion dogs. They are very intelligent and trainable.

German Shepherd

A loyal and protective breed that is used in the police force due to its high intelligence and trainability.

Beagle

A small and energetic breed known for its strong sense of smell. Beagles are usually playful.

poodle

An intelligent breed with a beautiful appearance that exists in different sizes. Poodles need regular care and grooming.

Bulldog

A short and muscular race with a special appearance. They are usually calm and kind and adapt well with children.

dachshund

A breed with short legs and an elongated body known for its brave and curious personality. They are usually loyal and playful.

Siberian husky dog

A breed with high energy and beautiful appearance, known for its strength and endurance. Huskies are usually sociable and loving.

Chihuahua

A small and energetic breed known for its strong personality. They are very dependent on their owner.

labrador

An intelligent and energetic breed that adapts well to the family. They are usually used for sports activities and hunting.

Rottweiler

A strong and protective breed known as guard dogs. Rottweilers are loyal and kind. But they need proper training.

shih tzu

A small breed with a beautiful appearance and a friendly personality. Shitzus usually love attention and live well in apartments.

1. Labrador retriever:
- Characteristics: Labradors usually have a friendly, kind and social personality. They easily adapt to children and other pets. - Needs: They need daily exercise and like activities such as swimming and running. Also, they need training and socialization.

2. German Shepherd:
- Characteristics: This breed is very loyal and protective. They have high intelligence and are easily trained. - Needs: they need practice and mental activity. Proper socialization from an early age is very important.

3. Beagle:
Characteristics: Beagles are known for their cheerful and playful personality. They are usually curious and independent. - Needs: they need sports and games. Also, they should be well socialized to avoid unwanted behaviors.

4. Poodle:
Characteristics: Poodles are very intelligent and trainable. They come in different sizes and usually have a friendly personality. - Needs: they need care and regular grooming. Daily exercise is also necessary to maintain their health.

5. Bulldog:
Characteristics: Bulldogs are usually calm and kind. They get along well with children and have a strong personality. - Requirements: They need moderate exercise. It may be necessary to take care of their health due to respiratory problems.

6. Dachshund:
Characteristics: Dachshunds are brave and curious and are known for their short legs and elongated body. - Needs: they need practice and mental activities. Socialization from an early age is important to prevent aggressive behavior.

7. Siberian husky dog:
Characteristics: Huskies are usually known for their high energy and beautiful appearance. They are social and playful. - Needs: they need a lot of exercise and should participate in physical activities such as running. Also, they need training and socialization.

8. Chihuahua:
- Characteristics: This breed is small but with a strong personality. Chihuahuas are usually attached to their owners. - Needs: They need little exercise, but should be considered in terms of socialization and proper training.

9. Colabrador:
Characteristics: Labradors are usually friendly, loyal and intelligent. They are used for sports activities and hunting. - Needs: They need daily exercise and must participate in social activities.

10. Rottweiler dog:
Characteristics: Rottweilers are strong and protective and usually very loyal to their families. - Needs: they need proper training and socialization to avoid aggressive behavior.

11. Shih Tzu dog:
- Characteristics: Shitzus are small, cute and friendly. They usually love attention and affection. - Needs: they need regular care of their hair and they need to participate in social activities.
